Password Reset Procedure

If a customer cannot log into their account, follow these steps:

1. Verify the customer's email address in the system
2. Click "Forgot Password" on the login page
3. Enter the registered email address
4. Check spam/junk folder if the reset email is not received within 5 minutes
5. The reset link expires after 24 hours
6. If the link expired, generate a new one from the admin panel under Users > Reset Password

Common issues:
- Corporate email filters may block the reset email. Ask IT to whitelist noreply@acmecorp.com
- If the customer's email is not found, the account may be registered under a different email
- Accounts locked after 5 failed login attempts require manual unlock by support admin
