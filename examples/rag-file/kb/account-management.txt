Account Management Procedures

Account types:
- Individual: Single user, personal email
- Team: Up to 10 users, shared workspace
- Enterprise: Unlimited users, SSO, dedicated support

Upgrading accounts:
1. Navigate to Settings > Subscription
2. Select desired plan
3. Changes take effect immediately
4. Pro-rated credit applied for remaining days on current plan

Downgrading accounts:
- Downgrades take effect at the end of the current billing period
- Features above the new tier limit are preserved but read-only
- Data exceeding new tier limits must be archived before downgrade

Closing accounts:
- All data is retained for 30 days after closure
- After 30 days, data is permanently deleted
- Reactivation within 30 days restores all data
- Contact support@acmecorp.com to initiate account closure

SSO configuration (Enterprise only):
- Supports SAML 2.0 and OpenID Connect
- Configure under Settings > Security > SSO
- Requires IdP metadata XML from your identity provider
- Test with a single user before enabling for all users
