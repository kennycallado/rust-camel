Billing and Refund Policy

Refund eligibility:
- Subscription charges: Full refund within 14 days of charge
- Duplicate charges: Immediate refund, no time limit
- Service outages: Pro-rata credit for downtime exceeding 4 hours

Processing refunds:
1. Locate the charge in the billing dashboard under Account > Transactions
2. Select the charge and click "Issue Refund"
3. Choose refund reason: duplicate, cancellation, service-issue, or other
4. Refunds appear on customer's statement within 5-10 business days
5. For charges older than 90 days, escalate to finance team via ticket #FIN-REFUND

Important notes:
- Never process refunds via direct bank transfer
- All refunds over $500 require manager approval
- Document the refund reason in the customer's CRM notes
